Manage your Store Tasks

Creating a task

As a new user, you can simply log in to your account and navigate to the "Create Task" section. Here, you will find a user-friendly interface where you can provide all the necessary details for your task. Enter the task name, description, assigned team members, deadline, and any other relevant information. Once you've filled in the required fields, click on the "Create" button, and voila! Your task will be successfully created and added to the task list. With our streamlined process, you can efficiently assign and manage tasks, ensuring smooth collaboration and productivity within your team.

Keep track of your tasks

Updating the status of a task

After logging into your account, navigate to the task list where you can view all your assigned tasks. Simply click on the task you want to update, and you'll be taken to its details page. Here, you'll find an option to change the status of the task. Choose the appropriate status from the provided options, such as "In Progress," "Completed," or "On Hold". Once you've made your selection, click on the "Update" button, and the task's status will be successfully updated. It's that simple!

Manage your team

Easy to work with

With the Task Manager, you have the ability to manage your team members effortlessly. As an administrator, you can easily edit their names or departments, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information. This functionality allows you to adapt to organizational changes and keep your team's records current. Additionally, if the need arises, you can selectively delete specific users from the system, streamlining your team management process. By having control over these aspects, you can maintain an organized and efficient team structure, ensuring smooth operations and effective collaboration within your organization.
